50th Anniversary of the Establishment of Japan-Nepal Diplomatic Relations

2021/3/22
The year 2006 marks the 50th Anniversary of the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations between Japan and Nepal. To celebrate the golden jubilee, a series of events was organized throughout the year with recognition that 50 years of friendship is indeed an epoch-making achievement and with determination to build a new chapter of friendship for another 50 years and beyond.
